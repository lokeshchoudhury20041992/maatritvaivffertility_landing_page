8 Reasons to Choose Maatritva IVF Centre

Advanced Embryology Lab

State-of-the-art embryology lab with time-lapse incubation, blastocyst culture, controlled temperature and air quality for optimal embryo development.

Expert, Experienced Doctors

Gold Medalist Dr. Ankita Mandal (MD, OBGYN, 12+ years) and Dr. Chhabi Ghosh (35+ years in ART) lead your care with international-level expertise.

Personalized Treatment Plans

No cookie-cutter protocols. Every patient gets a customized IVF plan based on their unique fertility profile, age, AMH, and medical history.

Transparent, Affordable Pricing

Full cost breakdown in writing before every procedure. No hidden charges. IVF packages starting from ₹95,000 with EMI options available.

Repeated IVF Failure Specialists

Specialized protocols for couples who have failed IVF elsewhere — including ERA testing, immunological workup, and individualized embryo selection.

PGT Genetic Testing

Preimplantation Genetic Testing (PGT-A) to screen embryos for chromosomal abnormalities before transfer, reducing miscarriage risk significantly.

Compassionate Patient Support

Dedicated patient counsellors, emotional support through treatment, nutritional guidance, and stress management — we care about you, not just your cycle.

Convenient Newtown Location

Located in Axismall Multicomplex, Newtown — easily accessible from Salt Lake, Rajarhat, and across North Kolkata.
