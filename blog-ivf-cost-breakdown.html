IVF Cost in Kolkata – Complete 2025 Breakdown

February 2025  ·  Dr. Ankita Mandal, MD (OBGYN)

IVF treatment cost in Kolkata can range from ₹90,000 to over ₹2,50,000 — and many couples are confused by why costs differ so dramatically between clinics. This guide breaks down every component of IVF pricing and explains what drives the differences.

The Real Components of IVF Cost

Cost Component Range (₹)
Basic IVF (stimulation + retrieval + transfer) ₹90,000 – ₹1,50,000
Ovarian stimulation medications ₹30,000 – ₹70,000 (extra)
ICSI procedure (if needed) +₹25,000 – ₹50,000
Blastocyst culture (Day 5) +₹15,000 – ₹25,000
PGT-A embryo testing +₹40,000 – ₹80,000
ERA test (receptivity) +₹25,000 – ₹40,000
Frozen embryo transfer (FET) ₹35,000 – ₹60,000
Monitoring scans & blood tests ₹8,000 – ₹20,000
Embryo freezing (annual storage) ₹10,000 – ₹20,000/year

Why IVF Costs Vary Between Clinics

  • Lab Technology: Time-lapse incubators, advanced culture media, and HEPA-filtered air increase costs but improve embryo quality.
  • Embryologist Experience: Skilled embryologists command higher clinical costs but significantly impact fertilization and embryo development rates.
  • Location: Hospital-attached clinics in prime locations cost more due to overhead.
  • Chain vs. Boutique: Large chains have fixed pricing; boutique clinics often personalize both treatment and costs.
  • What's Included: Some clinics quote the base IVF price only; always ask if medications and monitoring are included.
  • "Cheap IVF" Red Flags: Aggressively low-cost IVF packages may cut corners on lab quality or medication protocols. Always verify lab accreditation.

Questions to Ask Your Clinic Before Paying

  1. Is this an all-inclusive price or is medication extra?
  2. What happens if the cycle is cancelled due to poor response — do you refund or credit?
  3. Is embryo freezing included, and what is the annual storage fee?
  4. What additional procedures might be recommended, and their costs?
  5. Do you offer multi-cycle packages or EMI options?
