Fertility Care Across Kolkata

Maatritva IVF brings advanced fertility treatment closer to you with consultation centres across Kolkata's key neighbourhoods.

IVF Centre in Kolkata

Maatritva IVF serves patients from across Kolkata with advanced IVF, ICSI, IUI and fertility preservation services. Our city-wide network ensures expert reproductive care is never more than a short drive away.

IVF Centre in Newtown

Our flagship Newtown centre at Axis Mall Multicomplex is fully equipped with a modern embryology lab and ART suite. Walk-in consultations and end-to-end IVF treatment are available six days a week.

IVF Centre in Ballygunge

Patients in South Kolkata can meet our fertility specialists at the Ballygunge consultation centre. Initial assessments, scans and follow-up reviews happen here, with advanced procedures handled at our Newtown facility.

IVF Centre in Dum Dum

Couples from Dum Dum and the wider North Kolkata region access Maatritva's fertility services through our dedicated touchpoint. The Newtown ART centre is easily reachable via the EM Bypass and metro network.

IVF Centre in Garia

Maatritva IVF extends fertility consultations and follow-up care to patients in Garia and surrounding South Kolkata neighbourhoods. Comprehensive IVF cycles are conducted at our fully-equipped Newtown headquarters.
