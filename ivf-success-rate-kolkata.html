IVF Success Rate in Kolkata (2025)
By Age, Factors & What to Expect

Medically reviewed by Dr. Ankita Mandal, MD (OBGYN) — IVF Specialist

📌 Quick Answer

IVF success rate in Kolkata in 2025: Women under 35 achieve 55–70% live birth rate per transfer. Age 35–40: 40–50%. Above 40: 20–35%. These figures are per embryo transfer without PGT. Using blastocyst culture and PGT-A can push success rates higher. Cumulative rates across 3 cycles can reach up to 85% for women under 38.

Key Factors That Affect IVF Success

  • Maternal Age: The single most important determinant. Egg quality declines with age.
  • Egg Quality & Ovarian Reserve (AMH): Higher AMH means more eggs retrieved and better choices for transfer.
  • Sperm Quality: Morphology, motility, and DNA fragmentation index impact fertilization rates.
  • Uterine Health: Polyps, fibroids, or thin endometrium reduce implantation rates.
  • Embryology Lab Standards: Air quality, culture media, and incubator technology directly impact embryo viability.
  • Stimulation Protocol: Individualized protocols vs. routine ("cookie-cutter") approaches make a measurable difference.
  • Number of Eggs Retrieved: More mature eggs = better embryo selection = higher success chance.
  • Blastocyst vs Cleavage Stage Transfer: Day-5 blastocyst transfer generally yields higher implantation rates than Day-3.
  • PGT-A Testing: Eliminates chromosomally abnormal embryos, reducing miscarriage risk and improving live birth rates.

How to Improve Your IVF Success Chances

Before Treatment

  • Achieve healthy BMI (18.5–25)
  • Quit smoking and alcohol completely
  • Start prenatal vitamins (folic acid, Vitamin D)
  • Manage stress with yoga / meditation
  • Complete all pre-IVF workup tests

During Treatment

  • Strictly adhere to medication schedule
  • Attend all monitoring scans
  • Stay hydrated and rest adequately
  • Avoid strenuous exercise during stimulation
  • Follow luteal phase support instructions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the IVF success rate at Maatritva IVF Fertility Centre?

Maatritva IVF reports 55–70% success rates for women under 35 using personalized stimulation protocols and blastocyst transfer. Rates are comparable with the best centres in Kolkata.

Does IVF always work on the first try?

No. First-cycle success is not guaranteed. However, cumulative success rates improve with each subsequent cycle. Most couples achieve pregnancy within 1–3 cycles.

Can lifestyle changes improve IVF success?

Yes. Studies show that achieving a healthy BMI, quitting smoking, reducing alcohol, and managing stress can meaningfully improve IVF outcomes, particularly egg quality and uterine receptivity.

What is a "good" success rate for an IVF clinic?

For women under 35, a success rate of 50%+ per transfer is considered good. Be cautious of clinics claiming unusually high rates without age-stratified data — ask for broken-down statistics.
