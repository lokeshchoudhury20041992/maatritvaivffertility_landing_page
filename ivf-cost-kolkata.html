Factors That Affect IVF Cost in Kolkata

  • Patient Age: Older patients may need more stimulation medications or donor eggs, increasing costs.
  • Ovarian Reserve (AMH): Low AMH means more medications to stimulate egg production.
  • Number of Procedures: Adding ICSI, PGT, ERA, or blastocyst culture increases the base price.
  • Clinic Tier: Chain clinics vs. boutique fertility centres vary in pricing and service levels.
  • Number of Cycles: If multiple cycles are needed, costs multiply — look for multi-cycle packages.
  • Consultations and Diagnostics: Initial workup (blood tests, semen analysis, scan) may cost ₹5,000–₹15,000.

💡 Cost-Saving Tips

✅ Ask for an all-inclusive IVF package — medications, monitoring, retrieval, and transfer bundled. · ✅ Consider FET cycles if you have frozen embryos — much cheaper than a full IVF cycle. · ✅ Check for multi-cycle discounts at your clinic. · ✅ Compare 2–3 clinic quotes but never compromise on lab accreditation and doctor credentials.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is IVF covered by insurance in India?

IVF is not generally covered by health insurance in India. However, some corporate health insurance policies are beginning to include limited fertility coverage. Check your specific policy or ask your HR department.

Does the IVF cost include medications?

Usually not. Ovarian stimulation medications are billed separately and can add ₹30,000–₹70,000 to the total cost. Always ask for a medication-inclusive quote upfront.

Can I get IVF on EMI in Kolkata?

Yes. Many clinics including Maatritva IVF offer EMI options via bank partners or financial health loan providers. Ask about no-cost EMI plans during your consultation.
