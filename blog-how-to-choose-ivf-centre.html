How to Choose the Best IVF Centre in Kolkata — Expert Advice

February 2025  ·  Dr. Ankita Mandal, MD (OBGYN), IVF Specialist

Choosing the right IVF centre is one of the most important decisions of your fertility journey. The right clinic can significantly impact your success chances — and your emotional experience throughout. Here's a comprehensive checklist from an IVF specialist's perspective.

1. Verify the Embryology Lab's Quality

The embryology lab is the heart of any IVF clinic. A state-of-the-art lab uses:

  • HEPA-filtered, VOC-free air systems
  • Time-lapse incubators for continuous embryo monitoring
  • High-quality culture media (proven to support embryo development)
  • Experienced, certified embryologists

Ask: "Can you describe your embryology lab setup and your embryologist's qualifications?"

2. Check Doctor Credentials & Experience

Specialist Qualification

Look for MD (OBGYN) or DGO with fellowship in reproductive medicine or ART. A Gold Medal or academic track record is a positive marker.

Years of IVF Experience

Ask specifically how many IVF cycles the doctor has performed, not just years in practice. Look for 10+ years with a high case volume.

Complex Case Experience

Ask about experience with low AMH, PCOS, repeated IVF failure, advanced maternal age — these require specialized expertise.

3. Evaluate Pricing Transparency

Transparent pricing is a hallmark of an ethical clinic. A trustworthy clinic:

  • Provides a written, itemized cost breakdown before treatment starts
  • Does not significantly inflate quotes after initial consultation
  • Is clear about which procedures are included vs. extra
  • Offers fair cancellation/refund policies if a cycle is cancelled

Red flag: Clinics that refuse to give written quotes or whose price mysteriously increases at each appointment.

4. Look at Patient Reviews with Scepticism

Google reviews can be informative but must be read carefully:

  • Look for detailed reviews that mention the doctor by name and describe the experience
  • Beware of clinics with only 5-star reviews — a natural review profile includes some 4-star and mixed reviews
  • Check if the clinic responds to reviews (even negative ones) professionally

5. Red Flags to Avoid

Warning Signs

  • Guaranteed success rates — no ethical clinic guarantees IVF results
  • Pressure to start immediately without proper workup
  • No dedicated embryology lab on-site
  • Lack of individualized treatment — same protocol for everyone
  • Quotes that change dramatically between consultations
  • No post-transfer follow-up or emotional support offered

10 Questions to Ask Before Committing

  1. What is your success rate for my age group, specifically?
  2. How many IVF cycles has your embryologist performed this year?
  3. Is your embryology lab ICMR accredited?
  4. Is this quote all-inclusive? What's extra?
  5. What stimulation protocol will you use for me, and why?
  6. Do you offer blastocyst culture and PGT-A?
  7. What happens if my cycle is cancelled mid-way?
  8. How many embryos will you transfer, and what is your policy?
  9. What support do you offer during the 2-week wait?
  10. Who will I call in case of a concern at 11pm?
