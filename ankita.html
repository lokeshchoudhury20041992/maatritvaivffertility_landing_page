Clinical Expertise

Dr. Ankita Mandal specialises in the full spectrum of infertility evaluation and treatment. Her clinical expertise spans:

  • In Vitro Fertilisation (IVF) — complete cycle management from ovarian stimulation through egg retrieval, fertilisation, and embryo transfer
  • Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection (ICSI) — advanced micromanipulation technique for severe male-factor infertility, low sperm count, and failed conventional IVF
  • Intrauterine Insemination (IUI) — first-line infertility treatment for couples with unexplained or mild infertility
  • Ovulation Induction & Monitoring — for PCOS-related infertility and ovulatory dysfunction
  • Recurrent Pregnancy Loss Investigation & Management — hormonal, genetic, and anatomical work-up with targeted treatment plans
  • Comprehensive Gynaecological Care — as an expert Gynaecologist, managing PCOS, endometriosis, fibroids, menstrual disorders, and preventive womens health
  • Fertility Preservation — egg freezing (oocyte cryopreservation) and embryo banking for medical or elective reasons

Why Patients Choose Dr. Ankita Mandal

As a MBBS, MD, Diploma in Reproductive Medicine, FIMAS (G&O, Infertility & IVF specialist) at Maatritva IVF Fertility & Healthcare, Kolkata, Dr. Ankita Mandal brings a uniquely holistic approach to reproductive medicine. She was trained at premier institutions and has refined her expertise in IVF treatment in Kolkata, ICSI, and the management of all forms of female and male-factor infertility.

Patients seeking an IVF specialist in Kolkata or a skilled Gynaecologist often choose Dr. Ankita Mandal for her meticulous approach to diagnosis. She believes no two infertility journeys are identical, and therefore tailors every IVF or ICSI cycle to the individual patient's ovarian reserve, hormonal profile, and clinical history.

Her work at Maatritva IVF complements the clinic's state-of-the-art IVF laboratory in Newtown, Kolkata — where AI-assisted embryo selection, blastocyst culture, and Preimplantation Genetic Testing (PGT-A) maximise success rates. Whether you are exploring ICSI for male factor infertility, need expert guidance from a Gynaecologist for PCOS or endometriosis, or are preparing for your first IVF cycle, Dr. Ankita Mandal offers a compassionate, evidence-based consultation.
