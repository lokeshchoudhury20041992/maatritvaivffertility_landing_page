Why Choosing the Right IVF Centre in Kolkata Matters

Infertility affects 1 in 6 couples in India, and the success of IVF treatment depends heavily on the clinic you choose. Key differentiators include:

  • Embryology lab quality (air quality, culture media, incubator technology)
  • Doctor experience with complex cases (low AMH, repeated failures, advanced age)
  • Individualized treatment protocols — not cookie-cutter approaches
  • Advanced technology (ICSI, Blastocyst transfer, PGT-A, ERA test)
  • Transparent, honest pricing with no hidden charges

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Which IVF centre has the highest success rate in Kolkata?

Success rates vary by age and condition. Centres with advanced embryology labs and personalized treatment plans generally report higher success rates. Maatritva IVF reports 55–70% success for women under 35 using blastocyst transfer and individualized stimulation protocols.

What is the cost of IVF in Kolkata?

IVF treatment in Kolkata typically costs ₹90,000 to ₹2,50,000 per cycle. The exact cost depends on patient age, procedures needed (ICSI, PGT, ERA), and the clinic chosen. At Maatritva IVF, we offer transparent pricing with no hidden fees.

How many IVF cycles are usually required?

Most couples conceive within 1–3 IVF cycles. The cumulative success rate rises with each additional cycle. Age and embryo quality are the primary factors determining how many cycles are needed.

Is IVF painful?

IVF procedures are minimally invasive. Egg retrieval is performed under light sedation. Some women experience mild bloating or discomfort during ovarian stimulation, but this is temporary and well-managed with medication.

What is the best age to start IVF?

IVF success rates are highest for women under 35 (55–70%). Success declines with age — 40–50% for ages 35–40, and 20–35% above 40. Early consultation is always recommended if you have been trying to conceive for 12+ months (or 6 months if over 35).
