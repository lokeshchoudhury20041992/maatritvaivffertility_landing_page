Common Questions

What should I bring to my first appointment?
Please bring any previous medical records, ultrasound reports, and blood test results. If you have undergone previous fertility treatments, discharge summaries are very helpful.
How long does an IVF cycle take?
A typical IVF cycle takes about 4 to 6 weeks from the start of ovarian stimulation to the pregnancy test. However, timelines vary based on individual protocols.
Do you treat high-risk pregnancies?
Yes, Dr. Ghosh has extensive experience (35+ years) in managing high-risk pregnancies, including those with hypertension, diabetes, or history of recurrent miscarriage.
