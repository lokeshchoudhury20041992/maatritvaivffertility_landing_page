## 4. GMB Service List (identical across all 5 profiles)

Add each as a separate Service in the GMB Services section.

| Service Name | Description |
|---|---|
| **IVF (In Vitro Fertilization)** | Personalised IVF treatment with advanced embryology lab support, time-lapse incubation and blastocyst-stage transfer. Suitable for couples with tubal factor infertility, low AMH, unexplained infertility and repeated failures. |
| **IUI (Intrauterine Insemination)** | Office-based fertility treatment for mild male factor infertility, ovulation disorders and unexplained subfertility. Less invasive than IVF, suitable as a first-line option. |
| **ICSI (Intracytoplasmic Sperm Injection)** | Advanced IVF technique where a single sperm is injected directly into the egg. Recommended for severe male factor infertility, low sperm count and previous fertilisation failure. |
| **Egg Freezing (Oocyte Cryopreservation)** | Fertility preservation for women postponing pregnancy, undergoing cancer treatment or with declining ovarian reserve. Eggs are vitrified and stored for future use. |
| **Male Infertility Treatment** | Comprehensive workup including semen analysis, hormone testing and surgical options (TESA / micro-TESE). Treatment plans coordinated with andrology and ICSI as needed. |
