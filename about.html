About Our Clinic

A Boutique Sanctuary of Hope & Excellence

Maatritva IVF Fertility & Healthcare is a leading, patient-centric Assisted Reproductive Technology (ART) centre with a mission to provide personalised fertility care across Eastern India. We differentiate ourselves from the "factory" model of large chains by emphasizing a boutique, holistic approach. Our narrative of being a "sanctuary of hope" and providing "emotional wellness" is designed to appeal to patients who may feel overwhelmed by the impersonal nature of high-volume centres.

In-House Embryology: Our flagship state-of-the-art lab is located in Newtown's Axis Mall, ensuring continuity of care where the same team manages your entire cycle to reduce errors and improve confidence.

Multi-Nodal Network: Complemented by satellite clinics across Nagerbazar, Jadavpur, Garia, Lauhati, Deganga, Basirhat, Panskura, Ghatal, Arambag, Habra, Howrah, Sonarpur, and Barasat for localized patient accessibility.

Government Certified: Proudly ISO Certified & ICMR-Certified, a non-negotiable requirement for operational excellence under the Indian ART regulatory framework.

Everything You Need to Know

In addition to our flagship hub in Newtown (Axis Mall), our primary satellite clinics are located in Nagerbazar, Jadavpur, Garia, Barasat, and Ballygunge (at Suraksha Clinic via MOU). The strategic purpose of this multi-nodal, hub-and-spoke framework is to balance specialised, centralised laboratory excellence with localised, convenient patient accessibility.

Maatritva's primary technological differentiator is the use of AI-Assisted Embryo Selection alongside time-lapse imaging. This data-driven approach analyses thousands of data points to select the most viable embryo for transfer, significantly reducing the "time to pregnancy" and minimizing the physical and financial toll associated with fertility treatments.

  • IUI: Placement of washed, concentrated motile sperm near ovulation.
  • IVF: Controlled ovarian stimulation followed by extra-corporeal fertilisation and blastocyst culture.
  • ICSI: Direct injection of a single sperm into a mature oocyte (for severe male factor infertility).
  • Blastocyst Transfer: Culturing embryos to Day 5 for physiological synchrony.
  • eSET: Elective Single Embryo Transfer to maximize success while avoiding high-risk multiple pregnancies.

Our advantage is centered on a boutique, personalized, and holistic approach to fertility care. Unlike high-volume centers, we act as a "sanctuary of hope" providing meticulous, non-standardized treatment plans. By keeping our embryology lab strictly "in-house," we ensure continuity of care—where the same dedicated team handles your entire cycle—reducing the potential for errors and maximizing confidence.

Dr. Ankita Mandal: Gold medalist and Clinical Face with an MD in OBGYN and 15+ years of experience.
Dr. Chhabi Ghosh: Senior ART Specialist bringing over 35 years of critical expertise, especially for managing "difficult" cases and repeated IVF failures.
Dr. Prattay Ghosh: A General Physician (MD-Medicine), Clinical Cardiologist, and Diabetologist with 18+ years of experience. As infertility is often linked to metabolic disorders (like PCOS and insulin resistance), his integration into the team provides a unique, holistic approach to optimizing a patient's overall health before and during treatment.

In addition to AI-Assisted Embryo Selection, our lab offers Preimplantation Genetic Testing (PGT-A / Aneuploidy testing). This is particularly critical for patients of advanced maternal age or those with a history of recurrent pregnancy loss. Screening embryos for chromosomal abnormalities before transfer significantly lowers the incidence of miscarriages and improves overall success rates.

Maatritva is proudly ISO Certified and ICMR-Certified (Indian Council of Medical Research). These are non-negotiable requirements under the current Indian Assisted Reproductive Technology (ART) regulatory framework, verifying our compliance, safety, and operational excellence.
